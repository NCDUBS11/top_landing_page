Why Ethics Matter...

Rest assured, the members of 'Let's Grow!' are working to the best of their ability to provide you with plants of the highest quality via the most ethical means possible. Organic farming practices, the utilization of compostable pots, logistical reduction of "Plant Miles" and the use of peat alternatives are all key priorities at 'Let's Grow'. Please feel free to reach out to one of our team members via the 'Contact Us' link above if more information regarding production and procurement methods at 'Let's Grow!' are needed.
